Cloud Sketch

When I first tried to make the clouds move and add natural sound to my sketch, nothing felt right. The sound didn’t play at all at first, and the cloud movement looked too stiff and robotic. I wanted it to feel calm and natural, like real clouds drifting across the sky, but everything looked mechanical.

After experimenting for a while, I learned that I could use noise() instead of random() to make the movement more natural and smooth. I also found out that the sound didn’t play automatically because browsers block it unless the user clicks first. Once I added a simple “Click to start sound” message and used mousePressed() to play it, everything finally worked the way I wanted. The clouds started moving slowly and softly, and the background sound made the whole scene feel alive.
